About This Study

This research aimed to investigate how people evaluate recipes from different sources and how these evaluations might be influenced by nutritional information and recipe presentation.

Specifically, we were interested in understanding:

  • How people assess the healthiness and credibility of recipes
  • Whether there are differences in perception based on recipe categories
  • How nutritional information influences recipe evaluation
