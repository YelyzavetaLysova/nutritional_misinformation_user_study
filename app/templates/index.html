Recipe Survey Study

Welcome to Our Study!

Thank you for participating in our research on recipe preferences and perceptions. Your input is valuable to our study on nutritional understanding.

What You'll Do:

  1. Answer a few demographic questions
  2. Review and evaluate 5 different recipes
  3. Answer some final questions about your experience
  4. Read a brief debriefing about the study

The entire study will take approximately 10-15 minutes to complete.

Consent to Participate

By proceeding with this study, you agree that:

  • Your participation is voluntary
  • You can withdraw at any time
  • Your data will be collected anonymously
  • Your responses will be used for research purposes only
