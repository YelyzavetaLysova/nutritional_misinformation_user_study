## Recipe Evaluation Section
(Repeated for each of the 5 recipes)

Instructions: "Presented below is a recipe of a dish, with ingredients, instructions, and nutrients. Please read it carefully and afterwards answer the questions at the bottom of the page."

### 1. Clarity
Question: "How would you rate the clarity of this recipe?"
Scale: 1 (Not at all clear) to 7 (Very clear)

### 2. Completeness
Question: "How complete is this recipe?"
Scale: 1 (Very incomplete) to 7 (Very complete)

### 3. Overall Correctness
Question: "How correct is this recipe overall?"
Scale: 1 (Not at all correct) to 7 (Very correct)

### 4. Healthiness
Question: "How would you rate healthiness of this recipe?"
Scale: 1 (Not at all healthy) to 7 (Very healthy)

### 5. Tastiness
Question: "How tasty do you think this recipe would be?"
Scale: 1 (Not at all tasty) to 7 (Very tasty)

### 6. Component Complexity
Question: "Rate the complexness of each component: ingredients, instructions"

#### 6.1. Ingredients Complexity
Question: "Ingredients list complexness:"
Scale: 1 (Poor) to 7 (Excellent)

#### 6.2. Instructions Complexity
Question: "Instructions complexness:"
Scale: 1 (Poor) to 7 (Excellent)

### 7. Component Correctness
Question: "Please rate the correctness of each component:"

#### 7.1. Ingredients Correctness
Question: "Ingredients list correctness:"
Scale: 1 (Poor) to 7 (Excellent)

#### 7.2. Instructions Correctness
Question: "Instructions correctness:"
Scale: 1 (Poor) to 7 (Excellent)

#### 7.3. Nutrition Correctness
Question: "Nutrition information correctness:"
Scale: 1 (Poor) to 7 (Excellent)

### 8. Feasibility
Question: "How feasible would it be for you to make this recipe at home?"
Scale: 1 (Not at all feasible) to 7 (Very feasible)

### 9. Overall Quality
Question: "How would you rate the overall attractiveness of this recipe?"
Scale: 1 (Very poor) to 7 (Excellent)

### 10. Likelihood to Make
Question: "How likely it is that you would prepare this recipe?"
Scale: 1 (Very unlikely) to 7 (Very likely)

### 11. Additional Comments
Question: "Any additional comments about this recipe? (Optional)"
Response: Free-text field

## Post-Survey Section

### 1. Cooking Skills
Question: "How would you rate your cooking skills?"
Scale: 1 (Beginner) to 7 (Expert)

### 2. New Recipe Frequency
Question: "How often do you cook new recipes?"
Options:
- Very often - I try new recipes multiple times a week
- Often - I try new recipes weekly
- Sometimes - I try new recipes a couple times a month
- Rarely - I try new recipes a few times a year
- Never - I stick to recipes I already know

### 3. Recipe Decision Factors
Question: "What's most important to you when deciding whether to try a recipe?"
Options:
- Clear instructions
- Simple preparation
- Common ingredients I already have
- Nutritional value
- How the final dish will look

### 4. Home Cooking Frequency
Question: "How often do you cook meals at home?"
Options:
- Daily
- Several times a week
- About once a week
- A few times a month
- Rarely
- Never

### 5. Trust in Human Recipes
Question: "How much would you trust recipes created by human authors?"
Scale: 1 (Not at all) to 7 (Completely)

### 6. Trust in AI Recipes
Question: "How much would you trust recipes generated by AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT)?"
Scale: 1 (Not at all) to 7 (Completely)

### 7. AI Usage Frequency
Question: "How frequently do you use AI platforms like ChatGPT, DeepSeek, or Gemini Assistant?"
Options:
- Daily
- Several times a week
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Rarely
- Never used
- Not familiar with these platforms

### 8. Recipe Feasibility Comments
Question: "Any comments about what makes a recipe feasible for you to cook? (Optional)"
Response: Free-text field
